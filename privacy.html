1. Introduction

Silah Logistics Ltd. ("Company", "We", "Us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal information when you use our website (silahlogistics.com) and our freight forwarding platform (the "Platform").

2. Information We Collect

To provide efficient logistics services, we collect the following categories of data:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, job title, and phone number.
  • Company Data: Business name, VAT/Tax ID, registration number, and physical address.
  • Shipment Data: Origin and destination addresses, cargo descriptions, weight, and value (required to process quotes and bookings).
  • Financial Data: Payment methods and billing history (processed via secure third-party payment gateways).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage patterns collected via cookies and similar technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your data for the following legal bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide the logistics quotes and bookings you request.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve the Platform's performance, prevent fraud, and maintain security.
  • Compliance: To meet legal obligations, such as export control, anti-bribery, and tax reporting.
  • Consent: For marketing communications (which you can opt out of at any time).

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Because we act as an intermediary, your data is shared with:

  • Providers: Third-party carriers, warehouses, and customs brokers necessary to fulfill your shipping orders.
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (e.g., AWS/Google Cloud), analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), and payment processors.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect the safety and rights of Silah Logistics Ltd. or our users.

5. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in secure cloud regions globally. For international transfers, we use Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure your data remains protected.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

  • Access & Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Correction & Deletion: You may request that we fix inaccurate data or delete your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Objection: You may object to the processing of your data for direct marketing.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw any consent previously given for data processing.

7. Data Security and Retention

  • Security: We implement industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS for data in transit.
  • Retention: We keep shipment and account data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by financial and maritime regulations (typically 7 years).

8. Use of Cookies

We use "Essential Cookies" for site functionality and "Analytical Cookies" to understand how users interact with our Platform. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or our on-site cookie banner.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our Website and updating the "Effective Date."

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at: info@silahlogistics.com
